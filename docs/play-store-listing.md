## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Titan Protocol is a gamified personal operating system that turns your daily grind into a progression system. Track tasks across four engines — Body, Mind, Money, and Charisma — and watch your Titan Score climb as you build consistency.

FOUR ENGINES, ONE PROTOCOL
Every area of your life maps to an engine. Set missions for each, complete them daily, and earn XP. Your weighted Titan Score reflects how balanced and consistent you are across all four.

DAILY PROTOCOL
Start each morning with an intention. End each evening with a reflection. The protocol session keeps you accountable and tracks your streak — miss a day and the integrity system kicks in.

GAMIFICATION THAT MATTERS
- 8 rank tiers from Initiate to Titan
- 41 achievements across 5 rarity levels
- Weekly quests and boss challenges
- Skill trees with 80+ unlockable nodes
- 40+ earnable titles
- Field operations with stat bonuses

HABIT TRACKING WITH CHAINS
Build habits with streak chains. The 12-week heatmap shows your consistency at a glance. Chain breaks are tracked and recovery is rewarded — miss a day and the forgiving integrity system gives you a path back.

BUILT-IN TRACKERS
- Workout logger with exercises, sets, templates, and PRs
- Sleep tracker with quality scoring and debt calculation
- Weight tracker with moving averages and goal ETAs
- Nutrition tracker with macro targets and meal logging
- Budget manager with category limits and cashflow view
- Focus timer with Pomodoro sessions and deep work logging
- Journal with daily entries

NARRATIVE STORY ENGINE
Your journey has a story. 6 chapters unfold over 12 weeks with day-specific cinematics, daily briefings, and an archetype identity system. Choose from 8 archetypes — Titan, Athlete, Scholar, Hustler, Showman, Warrior, Founder, or Charmer — each with unique scoring weights.

CLOUD SYNCED
Your data syncs across devices via secure cloud storage. Every table is protected by row-level security — only you can see your data.

OFFLINE FIRST
The app works offline. Data is cached locally and syncs when you reconnect. No data loss.

DARK MODE ONLY
HUD-style dark interface with JetBrains Mono typography, engine-colored accents, and military-protocol aesthetics. Designed for focus, not distraction.

Free to use. No ads. Your data is yours.
