Titan Protocol — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

Titan Protocol ("the App") is a personal productivity and self-improvement application developed by Arun Sanjay ("we", "us"). This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account data. When you create an account we store your email address and a hashed password. If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google profile email. We do not store your Google password.

User-generated content. Tasks, habits, journal entries, goals, workout logs, sleep logs, weight logs, nutrition data, and other content you enter into the App are stored in our database so they sync across your devices.

Gamification data. XP, level, streak, achievements, quests, skill tree progress, and rank-up events are computed from your activity and stored alongside your profile.

Device-local preferences. Sound/haptic toggles, theme preferences, and onboarding flags are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Analytics. We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage events (screen views, feature usage, funnel progression). These events are tied to your user ID but do not include personal content (no task titles, journal text, or health data). You can request deletion of analytics data at any time.

Crash reports. We use Sentry to collect crash reports and error logs. These include device model, OS version, and stack traces. They do not include personal content.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and sync your data across devices
  • To compute gamification metrics (XP, streaks, achievements)
  • To improve app stability via crash reports
  • To understand feature usage and improve the product

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase (hosted on AWS ap-south-1). All database tables are protected by Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring you can only access your own data. Communication between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication (privacy policy)
  • PostHog — product analytics (privacy policy)
  • Sentry — error tracking (privacy policy)
  • Google Sign-In — optional OAuth authentication (privacy policy)

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete all your data at any time from the App's Settings screen ("Delete All Data"). This permanently removes your profile and all associated records from our database.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data within the App
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all data from Settings
  • Portability: Contact us to request an export of your data

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App and on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact
